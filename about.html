About My research focuses on computational biology, bioinformatics and software development applied to both systems and synthetic biology. My work covers genome-scale and kinetic metabolic modelling, synthetic DNA design, enzyme engineering, and 'omics data analysis, management and integration.

With degrees in chemistry and computing science, a PhD in computational systems biology and experience in both industrial and academic environments, I have over 20 years of interdisciplinary experience in software engineering, researching and developing computational approaches to solving biological problems.
